# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Arukikatha is a local Android walking timer. It does not require an account and does not send workout data to a server.

## Data Stored On Device

The app may store session history locally on your device, including:

- completed brisk walking rounds
- completed normal walking rounds
- successful minutes
- pause/reset counts
- whether the 30-minute goal was completed

This data stays on your device unless you manually share it outside the app.

## Data Collection

Arukikatha does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not use:

- analytics SDKs
- advertising SDKs
- cloud sync
- user accounts
- location tracking

## Permissions

Arukikatha may request or use Android permissions for:

- notifications, so an active timer can continue visibly while the app is in the background
- vibration, so mode transitions can be felt
- foreground service behavior, so the walking timer can keep running reliably during a session

## Contact

For questions about privacy, open an issue in the GitHub repository.
